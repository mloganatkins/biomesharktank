The Problem

The temperete grasslands cover over 8% of the world surface. They are found on every contenient, but only 3.4% of them are protected.

The Threats

Desertification

The process by which fertile land becomes desert.

  • Global warming is changing rainfall patterns
  • Row-crop agriculture is making all the soil in the grasslands unfertile because only one crop is planted and the soil nutrients are not replaced. Pests and disease spread easier when only one crop is present.
  • The grassland habitat is being built on to make urban areas.

My Pitch

I am asking for $250,000 to help save the North American Great Plains

The Solutions

Wind Breaks

Some of the money would be used to buy and plant trees as wind breaks in order to help prevent desertification. The trees would hold the soil together to prevent dust clouds from forming.

Education

Educate farmers on the benefits of crop rotation and returning nutrients to the soil. When the farmers dig up the sod to plant it relases CO2 into the air trapping more greenhouse gasses in our atmosphere

Dry Season Burning

Restores nutrents like calcium to the soil and helps promote new growth.

Workers

The money will also be used to hire workers to implement these strategies.
